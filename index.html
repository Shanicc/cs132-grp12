Tracing the Source

A project by Vanguard

A data science project that aims to identify the event(s) that played a key role in initiating and amplifying the misinformation about COVID-19 being created by China. This research holds significance as it further aims to combat the spread of false information and foster a more informed society. It is situated within the context of the global pandemic and the significant impact misinformation can have on public health, international relations, and societal trust.




Here's our problem formulation.

Research Question

What event has triggered the spread of misinformation about Covid-19 being created by China?

Hypothesis

The timing of the Wuhan lab virus leakage rumors correlates with the spread of misinformation about Covid-19 being created by China.

Action Plan

Analyze the frequency and posting time of misinformation tweets about Covid-19 being created by China.
